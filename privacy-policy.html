Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Overview

Gold Worth – Gold & Silver Price Tracker values your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All user data, such as portfolio details, alerts, and settings, are stored securely in your browser’s chrome.storage.local.

3. Data Usage

The extension only accesses public APIs to fetch market data and verify premium licenses:

  • https://api.gold-api.com – for live gold and silver prices
  • https://api.frankfurter.dev – for currency exchange rates
  • https://api.gumroad.com – for license verification

These APIs do not access or store any personal user data.

4. Data Storage & Retention

All user data remains stored locally on your device. You may remove it anytime by uninstalling the extension.

5. Permissions Justification

  • storage – Save portfolio, alerts, and user settings locally.
  • alarms – Automatically update metal prices and check alerts on schedule.
  • notifications – Show price and goal alerts to the user.

6. Third-Party Access

No personal data is shared with any third parties. External API access is limited to fetching public market data.
